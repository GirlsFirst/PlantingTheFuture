PLANTS

succulent:

- good light

- avoid direct sunlight.

watering:

- most die from overwatering

- only water when the compost feels dry to the touch

Snake plants

- grow in good light

- away from direct sunlight

- grow in gritty, free-draining compost

watering

- allow to dry out completely between watering

money plant

- a type of succulent, it can grow very large

- likes sunny position

- avoid midday sun

watering

- water occasionally

Peace lilly

- good light

- avoid direct sunlight

- watering

- most die from overwatering

- only water when the compost feels dry to the touch

fern

- grow in cool

- shady spots such as bathroom of kitchen

watering

- keep compost moist at all times

- mist foliage regularly with a hand sprayer

rubber plant

- keep in position with plenty of light

- keep away from direct sunlight

watering

- water when compost feels dry

- allow excess water to drain from bottom of pot
