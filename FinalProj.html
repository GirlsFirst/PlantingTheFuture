Vermicomposting

What is Vermicomposting?

Using earthworms to convert organic waste into fertilizer

Why use this method?

- Allows organic waste to break down into nutrient-rich compost to grow new plants

- Stops water contamination

How it Works?

What is Planting?

Why Plant?

- Using photosynthesis, they produce oxygen

- Provide fuel that is used by many animals as food
