succulents

- good light

- avoid direct sunlight

watering:

- most die from overwatering

- only water when the compost feels dry to the touch

aloe vera

- keep in in a spot with direct sunlight

- giving it more direct sunlight helps it grow better

watering

- can survive in very dry conditions

- in the summer months soak the soil to water your aloe

jade plant

- keep in full, direct sunlight to grow properly

- try to keep close to a bright window

watering

- keep soil moist

- you don't need it to dry out too much

- water whenever soil feels dry to the touch

rubber plant

- keep in position with plenty of light

- keep away from direct sunlight

watering

- water when compost feels dry

- allow excess water to drain from bottom of pot

snake plant

- grow in good light

- away from direct sunlight

- grow in gritty, free-draining compost

watering

- allow to dry out completely between watering

cactus

- are happiest in a really sunny spot

- they need a very gritty and free-draining soilt

watering

- only very occasional watering
